<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled Steel Plate</li><li>Cold Rolled Steel Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy steel plate market comprises primarily two types: hot rolled steel plates and cold rolled steel plates. Hot rolled steel plates are produced by heating steel above its recrystallization temperature, allowing for easier shaping and forming, typically resulting in a rougher surface finish. In contrast, cold rolled steel plates are processed at room temperature, offering a smoother surface and tighter tolerances, making them suitable for precision applications. Both types are essential across various industries, including construction and manufacturing.</p></p>
