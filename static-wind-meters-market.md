<p><strong>The Static Wind Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-beam</li><li>Reflective</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Static wind meters can be categorized into two main types: through-beam and reflective. Through-beam wind meters utilize a pair of sensors, where one emits a beam and the other detects it, measuring wind speed by analyzing the disruption of the beam. Reflective wind meters, on the other hand, use a sensor that reflects emitted signals off wind particles to calculate speed and direction. Each type serves specific applications, providing critical data for wind measurement in various industries.</p></p>
