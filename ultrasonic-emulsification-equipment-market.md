Oil-in-water-in-oil (O/W/O) emulsions feature oil droplets dispersed in water, relevant for cosmetics and food products. Other emulsions encompass various formulations tailored for specific industrial needs, such as pharmaceuticals and chemical processing, highlighting the versatility of ultrasonic technology in diverse applications.</p></p>
