<p><strong>The EP Catheter Ablation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radiofrequency Ablation Catheters</li><li>Cryoablation EP Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EP catheter ablation market primarily consists of two types: radiofrequency ablation catheters and cryoablation EP catheters. Radiofrequency ablation catheters utilize heat generated by radio waves to destroy targeted cardiac tissue, effectively treating arrhythmias. In contrast, cryoablation EP catheters employ extreme cold to achieve tissue destruction, offering a different mechanism for managing heart rhythm disorders. Both techniques are integral to electrophysiology procedures, catering to various clinical needs and patient preferences in the management of cardiac conditions.</p></p>
