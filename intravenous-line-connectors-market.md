<p><strong>The Intravenous Line Connectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Negative Fluid Displacement Intravenous Lineconnectors</li><li>Positive Fluid Displacement Intravenous Lineconnectors</li><li>Neutral Fluid Displacement Intravenous Lineconnectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intravenous line connectors market includes three main types: Negative Fluid Displacement, Positive Fluid Displacement, and Neutral Fluid Displacement connectors. Negative Fluid Displacement connectors minimize the amount of blood left in the line, reducing infection risk. Positive Fluid Displacement connectors require a deliberate action to push fluid backward, ensuring reliable filling and medication delivery. Neutral Fluid Displacement connectors maintain equilibrium and minimize backflow while allowing for easy access and fluid exchange, providing varied options for medical applications and enhancing patient safety.</p></p>
