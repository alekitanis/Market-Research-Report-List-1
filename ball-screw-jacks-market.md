<p><p>The ball screw jacks market comprises two primary types: worm gear ball screw jacks and electro-mechanical screw jacks. Worm gear ball screw jacks utilize a worm gear mechanism to convert rotational motion into linear motion, offering high torque and precision, making them suitable for heavy lifting applications. Electro-mechanical screw jacks, on the other hand, leverage electric motors to drive the screw, providing efficient and precise control over motion. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, including automation and material handling.</p></p>
