<p><p>The baking enzyme market includes various types that enhance dough characteristics and improve product quality. Amylase breaks down starch into sugars, promoting fermentation and improving texture. Protease modifies gluten strength and enhances dough extensibility. Cellulase breaks down cellulose, improving dough stability and gas retention. Oxidase contributes to dough strength and color by strengthening gluten networks through oxidation. Other enzymes may include lipases and xylanases, which further optimize baking processes, enhance shelf life, and improve overall quality and flavor.</p></p>
