<p><p>The long microfiber market is categorized into two main types based on denier measurements: 0.3-1.0 Dtex and above 1.0 Dtex. The 0.3-1.0 Dtex segment features finer fibers, offering enhanced softness, lightweight properties, and improved water absorption, making it ideal for applications like delicate textiles and filters. In contrast, the above 1.0 Dtex segment encompasses thicker fibers, providing greater durability and strength, suitable for products requiring higher resistance, such as industrial textiles and robust cleaning materials. Both categories cater to diverse consumer needs across various industries.</p></p>
