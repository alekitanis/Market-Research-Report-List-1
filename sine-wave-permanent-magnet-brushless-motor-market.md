<p><p>The Sine Wave Permanent Magnet Brushless Motor market includes two primary types: Built-in Type Motors and Surface Mount Type Motors. Built-in Type Motors feature a rotor embedded within the stator, allowing for a compact design and efficient thermal management, making them ideal for limited spaces. Conversely, Surface Mount Type Motors have permanent magnets mounted on the rotor's surface, providing higher torque and efficiency. Both types cater to diverse applications, from consumer electronics to industrial machinery, enhancing performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
