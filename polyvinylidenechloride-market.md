<p><p>The polyvinylidene chloride (PVDC) market is primarily divided into two types: PVDC resins and PVDC latex. PVDC resins are solid polymers known for their excellent barrier properties, making them ideal for packaging applications that require protection against moisture and gases. On the other hand, PVDC latex is a water-based dispersion used in coatings and adhesives, offering flexibility and compatibility with various substrates. Both types are essential in industries such as food packaging, pharmaceuticals, and construction, emphasizing their versatility.</p></p>
