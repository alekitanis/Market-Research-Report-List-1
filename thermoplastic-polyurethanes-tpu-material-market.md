<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyurethanes (TPU) are versatile materials categorized into three primary types: Polyester-based TPUs, known for their excellent mechanical properties and resistance to abrasion; Polyether-based TPUs, which offer superior flexibility and hydrolytic stability; and Polycaprolactone TPUs, recognized for their biodegradability and softer texture. Each type serves unique applications, with polyester suitable for automotive and industrial uses, polyether favored in textile and medical fields, and polycaprolactone ideal for environmentally friendly products and applications requiring elastic properties.</p></p>
