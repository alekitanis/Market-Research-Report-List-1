<p><strong>The Encapsulated Flavors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluid Bed Coating</li><li>Spray Drying</li><li>Glass Encapsulation</li><li>Spray Chilling/Congealing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The encapsulated flavors market is categorized into several types, including fluid bed coating, spray drying, glass encapsulation, and spray chilling/congealing. Fluid bed coating involves applying a protective layer around flavor particles for controlled release. Spray drying transforms liquid flavors into powders, enhancing stability. Glass encapsulation uses a glass matrix to safeguard flavors from environmental factors. Spray chilling/congealing quickly cools liquid droplets, forming solid flavor encapsulates. Each method offers unique benefits for flavor preservation and release in various food applications.</p></p>
