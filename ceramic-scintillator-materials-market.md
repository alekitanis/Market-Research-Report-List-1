<p><strong>The Ceramic Scintillator Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi Crystal</li><li>Single Crystal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic scintillator materials market is primarily divided into two types: multi-crystal and single crystal. Multi-crystal scintillators consist of numerous small crystals that combine to form a larger structure, offering benefits like lower production costs and easier fabrication. In contrast, single crystal scintillators are made from a single, continuous crystal, providing superior light yield and energy resolution, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Each type serves distinct needs within fields such as radiation detection and medical imaging.</p></p>
