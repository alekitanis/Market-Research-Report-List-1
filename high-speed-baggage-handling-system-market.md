<p><p>The high-speed baggage handling system market features two primary types: RFID baggage transport and sortation systems and barcode baggage transport and sortation systems. RFID systems utilize radio frequency identification technology for real-time tracking and seamless sorting, enhancing efficiency and accuracy. In contrast, barcode systems rely on printed barcodes scanned at various checkpoints, facilitating baggage tracking. Both systems aim to streamline airport operations, reduce handling time, and minimize lost luggage incidents, catering to the growing demands of air travel.</p></p>
