<p><p>The needle-based biopsy gun market encompasses various techniques for tissue sampling. Vacuum-assisted biopsy uses suction to obtain larger tissue samples, enabling more accurate diagnoses. Fine needle aspiration employs a thin, hollow needle to extract small tissue samples, offering minimally invasive options for conditions like cancer. Core needle biopsy involves a larger, hollow needle that captures core samples of tissue, providing definitive information about the structure and nature of a lesion. Each type caters to different clinical needs and diagnostic requirements.</p></p>
