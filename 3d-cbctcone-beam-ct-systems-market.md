<p><p>3D CBCT (Cone Beam Computed Tomography) systems are advanced imaging technologies that provide high-resolution, three-dimensional images of dental and maxillofacial structures. These systems utilize a cone-shaped X-ray beam to capture detailed images with significantly lower radiation exposure compared to traditional CT scans. This technology is particularly valuable in fields such as dentistry, orthodontics, and otolaryngology, facilitating precise diagnostics and treatment planning.</p><p>The 3D CBCT/Cone Beam CT Systems Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.6% during the forecast period, driven by rising demand for non-invasive imaging techniques and a growing emphasis on early diagnosis and treatment in various medical fields. Moreover, the expanding applications of CBCT in surgical planning, implant placement, and orthodontic assessments are further propelling market growth.
