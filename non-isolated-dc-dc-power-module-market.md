<p><p>The non-isolated DC-DC power module market primarily consists of two types: buck and boost converters. Buck converters efficiently step down voltage levels, making them ideal for applications requiring lower voltage from a higher input. Conversely, boost converters increase voltage levels, enabling devices to operate at higher potentials from a lower input source. Both types are essential in various electronic applications, including power supplies, battery management systems, and renewable energy systems, due to their efficiency and compact design.</p></p>
