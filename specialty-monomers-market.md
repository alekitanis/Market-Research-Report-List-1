Acrylate monomers are widely used in adhesives, coatings, and sealants due to their excellent adhesion properties. Methacrylate monomers find applications in cements and resins, offering enhanced thermal stability. Acrylamide monomers are primarily utilized in the production of polyacrylamide, essential for water treatment and soil conditioning. The "Other" category encompasses niche monomers that cater to specific industrial needs, expanding the market's diversity.</p></p>
